# 💬 Confirmation Messages: Your Consent Matters! ✨

Welcome to the guide on **Confirmation Messages** in OStimNet! 💖 We know that when NPCs start getting handsy or want to switch things up, you want to stay in control! That's exactly what this feature is for. Let's dive in! 🚀

> ⚠️ **When you see Skyrim message boxes NEVER use mouse to answer them, it will make sometimes your camera to fly away, it is known issue without known solution. Use keyboard hotkeys to select confirmation option.**

## 🤔 What Are Confirmation Messages?

Whenever an NPC wants to start a fun time, join an ongoing scene, change positions, or even stop what's happening, the game will pause and pop up a **Confirmation Message** on your screen! 🛑✋

This gives *you* the power to decide what happens next. You get to be the director of your own story! 🎬✨

## 🎮 What Are Your Options?

When a confirmation box pops up, you'll see a message explaining exactly what the NPC wants to do (like starting a scene or changing the pace). You'll usually have three super simple options:

1. ✅ **Accept**
   You're down for it! The action continues exactly as the NPC requested. Have fun! 🎉

2. ❌ **Cancel**
   Nope, not right now! You reject the request, and the NPC will back off, no event of decline or even attempt to use action will be written in SkyrimNet's events 🛑

3. ✍️ **Decline (Explain)**
   This one is special! ✨ If you want to say no *and* tell them exactly why, choose this! A SkyrimNet's text box will pop up so you can type your reason (e.g., "I have a headache," or "Not in front of the Jarl!"). Your customized explanation is sent straight into the story engine! 🧠💬

## ⚙️ Cool Settings You Should Know About!

Want to tweak how these messages work? Head over to the settings! 🛠️ Here are the configs you can play with to customize your experience:

- ⏳ **[Actions cooldown (seconds)](Config.md#actions-cooldown-seconds-default-20s)**
  How long should an NPC wait before they can ask to do something again after a normal interaction? (Prevents them from spamming you! 🛑)

- 🥶 **[Actions decline cooldown (seconds)](Config.md#actions-decline-cooldown-seconds-default-40s)**
  If you reject an NPC, how long are they put in the "timeout corner" before they can try asking again? Set this higher if they are being too persistent! 🙅‍♀️

- 👻 **[Confirmation modal for NPC-only scenes](Config.md#confirmation-modal-for-npc-only-scenes-default-off)**
  By default, if you aren't involved in the scene, the mod just lets the NPCs do their thing. But if you turn this on, you'll get a prompt even when NPCs are trying to hook up with *each other* without you! 🍿👀

- 😈 **[Show confirmation modal for actions with aggressive intent](Config.md#show-confirmation-modal-for-actions-with-aggressive-intent-default-off)**
  If a scene has an "Aggressive" intent, the mod normally skips asking you and just forces the action. Turn this setting **ON** if you still want to be prompted and have a say, even in these situations! 🛡️⚔️
